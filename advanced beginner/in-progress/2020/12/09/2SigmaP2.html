  • price: the larger stdv on price means the rent data is widely spread out making it less reliable. the data shows that an apartment costs \$43 per month which is ridiculously cheap and another costs over \$4M per month. for accurate prediction purposes, it is essential that we achieve a more centered price distribution.
  • latitude longitude: the apartments are located in New York City (40.730610,-73.935242). mapping out the locations from the data with the coordinates (0.0,XXX) and (XXX,0.0) are locations found nowhere near New York City.
  • bedrooms and bathrooms: apartments normally do not have 8 bedrooms or 10 bathrooms.

    from the above summary statistics, we can conclude that our data has some serious anomalies.
